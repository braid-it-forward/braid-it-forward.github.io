Our Mission

Growing up is hard for anyone. Finding confidence can be difficult, but we found that hair can change everything.

Beautiful hair gives any girl the confidence to be herself. It's the spark that I saw in my sister J's eyes that made me want to give this to other girls just like her.

At Braid it Forward, we provide affordable braiding for any girl in the foster care system who needs it.

You'll see the difference instantly, so please, reach out for more info! We can't wait to meet you!!

Who am I?

Hey! I'm Jasmine!! I'm a Social Work student at UW Madison. My family has been a foster family for many years and I've had the privilege of helping to care for so many amazing and sweet kids.

Besides school and helping with all of our placements, I love all things girlie: makeup, hair, bows, nails, cute outfits, and everything else! This inspired me to start Braid it Forward.

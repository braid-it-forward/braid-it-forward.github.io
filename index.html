Our Mission

Growing up is hard for anyone. Finding confidence can be difficult, but we found that hair can change everything.

Beautiful hair gives any girl the confidence to be herself. It's the spark that I saw in my sister J's eyes that made me want to give this to other girls just like her.

At Braid it Forward, we provide affordable braiding for any girl in the foster care system who needs it. Everything from twists to cornrows, we want to give girls the confidence to be themselves.

You'll see the difference instantly, so please, reach out for more info! We can't wait to meet you!!

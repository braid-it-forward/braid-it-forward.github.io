Our Mission

Growing up is hard for anyone. Finding confidence can be difficult, but we found that hair can change everything.

Beautiful hair gives any little girl the confidence to be herself. It's the spark that I saw in my sister J's eyes that made me want to give this to other girls just like her.

At Braid it Forward, we provide free braiding for any young girl in the foster care system who needs it. Everything from twists to cornrows, we want to give little girls the confidence to be themselves.

The ear-to-ear smile and the little strut say it all. You'll see the difference instantly, so please, reach out for more info! We can't wait to meet you!!
